THE BAOBAB ABSTRACT INTRODUCTION METHODS RESULTS DISCUSSION THE ARTIST VIDEO ESSAY

THE BAOBAB IS COMPRISED OF SIX ELEMENTS, EACH OF WHICH SYMBOLICALLY GESTURES TO THE MAIN THEMES OF THE COLLECTION: NATURE, DEATH, CONTROL, KNOWLEDGE AND TIME.
CONSTRAINED BORDER:
MAN'S CONTROL OVER NATURE.
UNCONSTRAINED BORDER:
NATURE'S CONTROL OVER MAN.
WHITE PATCHES:
TIME AND DECAY.
UNIFORM CURVES:
KNOWLEDGE AND UNDERSTANDING.
NONUNIFORM CURVES:
CONFUSION AND MISUNDERSTANDING.
MAGNIFYING LENS:
MAN'S EFFORTS TO RESEARCH AND LEARN.

THREE COMPOSITIONS, TITLED 'POWER', 'DISCOVERY' AND 'DECAY', ARE GENERATED THROUGH A SPECIFIC SELECTION AND SEQUENCE OF THE ABOVE SIX ELEMENTS, PROGRAMMATICALLY INTERWOVEN TOGETHER THROUGH ADVANCED TRIGONOMETRIC GRAPHING TECHNIQUES. FOR EXAMPLE, THE COMPOSITION 'POWER' IS GENERATED WITH ELEMENTS: B, A, C AND D. THE SELECTION OF ELEMENTS FOR EACH COMPOSITION IS INTENTIONAL, IN ORDER TO TELL A STORY.
