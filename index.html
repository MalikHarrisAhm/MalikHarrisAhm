 Hello, my name is Malik, I am a 24-year-old generative artist raised just outside London, England. I have a Masters degree in Pharmacy from the University of Manchester and I have been programming for 4 years, with a speciality in artificial intelligence and healthcare technology. I am Founder and CEO of Dosewolf, a medication tracking app that allows you to adjust your dose times as you go, something that is important for time-sensitive medication. I am also Founder and Executive Director of a non-profit called OpenPIL. OpenPIL is an open source clinical drug information database, built entirely by an AI developed by myself. The aim is to free all drug information, which is currently riddled with copyright restrictions; the organisation has won a prestigious award from the University of Manchester. The OpenPIL AI is open source and it can be found on GitHub or through the OpenPIL website.

I have always had a passion for art and design. To bring this into my work, I develop AI models which create one-of-a-kind 3D generative art pieces. My most recent and first public collection is called Manic Cubes.
